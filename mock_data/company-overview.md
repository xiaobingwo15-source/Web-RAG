# IE Industrial Technology — Company Overview

## About Us

IE Industrial Technology Co., Ltd. is a leading provider of smart manufacturing solutions, founded in 2008 and headquartered in Shenzhen, China. We specialize in industrial automation, IoT-enabled equipment, and data-driven factory optimization. Our mission is to empower manufacturers with intelligent, connected, and efficient production systems.

## Company Snapshot

- **Founded**: 2008
- **Headquarters**: Nanshan District, Shenzhen, Guangdong, China
- **Global Offices**: Shanghai, Suzhou, Munich, Detroit, Tokyo
- **Employees**: 1,200+ worldwide
- **Revenue (2025)**: RMB 2.3 billion
- **Stock Code**: SZSE 300888

## Mission Statement

To accelerate the transition to Industry 4.0 by delivering reliable, scalable, and intelligent automation platforms that reduce operational costs and improve production quality for manufacturers worldwide.

## Core Values

1. **Innovation First** — We invest 18% of annual revenue in R&D to stay at the forefront of industrial IoT and automation technology.
2. **Customer Success** — Our solutions are designed around real factory floor challenges, not theoretical use cases.
3. **Reliability** — Industrial-grade quality is non-negotiable. Our products carry ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and IEC 62443 certifications.
4. **Collaboration** — We partner with leading universities (Tsinghua, TU Munich, MIT) and industry consortia to advance smart manufacturing standards.

## Milestones

- **2008**: Founded in Shenzhen with 12 employees, focused on PLC controllers
- **2012**: Launched first-generation SCADA system for South China manufacturers
- **2015**: Expanded to Shanghai and Suzhou; crossed RMB 500M revenue
- **2017**: Acquired SensorTech GmbH (Munich), entering the European market
- **2019**: Launched SmartEdge IoT platform — our flagship cloud-to-edge solution
- **2021**: IPO on Shenzhen Stock Exchange (ChiNext board)
- **2023**: Opened Detroit office to serve North American automotive clients
- **2024**: Crossed 1,000 employees; launched AI-powered predictive maintenance module
- **2025**: Revenue reached RMB 2.3 billion; named "Top 50 Smart Manufacturing Companies in Asia" by Industry Week
