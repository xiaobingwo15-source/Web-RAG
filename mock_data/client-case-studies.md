## Case Study 1: BYD Automotive — Shenzhen Factory Automation

### Client Profile
- **Industry**: Electric vehicle manufacturing
- **Location**: Shenzhen, Guangdong, China
- **Scale**: 12 production lines, 3,200 workers, 800+ robots

### Challenge
BYD's Shenzhen plant needed to reduce battery pack assembly defects and improve line throughput to meet surging EV demand. The existing inspection system relied on manual visual checks, catching only 78% of defects with a 15% false positive rate. Unplanned downtime on critical welding stations averaged 12 hours per month.

### Solution
IE deployed a comprehensive SmartEdge-based solution:
- **48 IE-Vision M units** for automated battery cell inspection and weld seam verification
- **SmartEdge SE-Enterprise** connecting 1,200 sensors and 800 robots across 12 lines
- **IE-Predict Professional** monitoring 60 critical welding stations and CNC machines
- **IE-PLC 500** controllers replacing legacy Siemens S7-300 on 4 lines

### Results (12 months after deployment)

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Defect detection rate | 78% | 99.2% | +21.2 pp |
| False positive rate | 15% | 1.8% | -13.2 pp |
| Unplanned downtime | 12 hrs/month | 2.1 hrs/month | -82.5% |
| Line throughput | 42 units/hour | 51 units/hour | +21.4% |
| Annual savings | — | RMB 18.5M | — |

### Client Quote
> "SmartEdge gave us eyes on every station and predictions on every critical machine. We went from firefighting to optimizing." — **Zhang Wei, VP of Manufacturing, BYD**

## Case Study 2: Mengniu Dairy — Smart Packaging Line

### Client Profile
- **Industry**: Food & beverage (dairy products)
- **Location**: Hohhot, Inner Mongolia, China
- **Scale**: 6 packaging lines, 24/7 operation

### Challenge
Mengniu's packaging lines experienced frequent label misalignment and fill-level deviations, causing 3.2% waste rate. The quality team needed real-time visibility into line performance and faster root-cause analysis when issues occurred. Regulatory compliance required full batch traceability.

| Annual savings | — | RMB 8.2M | — |

## Case Study 3: TSMC (Taiwan Semiconductor) — Equipment Health Monitoring

### Client Profile
- **Industry**: Semiconductor fabrication
- **Location**: Tainan, Taiwan
- **Scale**: Cleanroom environment, 200+ critical process tools

### Challenge
TSMC's Tainan Fab 18 needed to reduce unplanned tool downtime in their advanced 5nm process line. A single hour of unplanned downtime on a lithography tool costs approximately USD 150,000 in lost wafers. Existing maintenance was purely schedule-based, leading to both over-maintenance and unexpected failures.

### Solution
- **SmartEdge SE-Enterprise** with custom vibration and thermal sensor arrays on 50 critical tools
- **IE-Predict Enterprise** with custom ML models trained on 18 months of historical tool data
- **Digital twin integration** for remaining useful life (RUL) prediction
- On-premise deployment with air-gapped security (no cloud connectivity)

### Results (18 months after deployment)

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Unplanned downtime events | 8/month | 1.2/month | -85% |
| Mean time to detect anomaly | 4.2 hours | 8 minutes | -96.8% |
| Maintenance cost | $2.1M/year | $1.4M/year | -33.3% |
| Tool availability | 92.5% | 97.8% | +5.3 pp |
| Estimated annual savings | — | USD 12.8M | — |

### Client Quote
> "The predictive models caught a bearing degradation in our EUV scanner 3 weeks before it would have failed. That single catch saved us over $2M in potential wafer losses." — **Dr. Chen, Fab 18 Engineering Director**

## Case Study 4: Nestlé China — Energy Optimization

### Client Profile
- **Industry**: Food manufacturing
- **Location**: Tianjin and Dongguan, China
- **Scale**: 2 factories, 8 production lines total

### Challenge
Nestlé China's two factories had energy costs of RMB 42M/year with no granular visibility into which lines or machines consumed the most. Corporate sustainability targets required a 20% energy reduction by 2027. Existing meters only tracked factory-level consumption.

### Solution
- **400+ IE-Sense C500 current sensors** on individual motors, compressors, and HVAC units
- **100+ IE-Sense T100 temperature sensors** for oven and chiller monitoring
- **SmartEdge SE-Pro** on each factory with energy analytics dashboard
- **Custom energy optimization model** using IE-Predict platform
- Integration with building management system (BMS) for automated load shifting

### Results (9 months after deployment)

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Energy consumption | 42M RMB/year | 35.7M RMB/year | -15% |
| Peak demand charges | RMB 8.4M/year | RMB 6.1M/year | -27.4% |
| CO2 emissions | 12,500 tons/year | 10,625 tons/year | -15% |
| ROI payback period | — | 14 months | — |

### Client Quote
> "We went from guessing where energy was wasted to knowing exactly which compressor on which line was running inefficiently. The 15% reduction in just 9 months exceeded our expectations." — **Wang Jun, Facilities Director, Nestlé China**
